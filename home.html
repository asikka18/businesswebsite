Welcome to EcoSteps!

Founded by environmental enthusiast and educator Sarah Whitmore, EcoSteps is a non-profit dedicated to empowering individuals to make sustainable choices in their daily lives. Our mission is to provide accessible resources, tips, and community support to help everyone take small steps towards a greener, more eco-friendly lifestyle.

Our Vision

At EcoSteps, we believe that every action counts. Whether it's reducing plastic use, conserving water, or supporting renewable energy, our goal is to show how small, mindful decisions can create a significant impact when done collectively. We focus on practical, simple, and affordable ways to live more sustainably.

Show Your Support

Your Support Makes a Difference!

Every dollar you donate to EcoSteps directly funds our community projects and educational initiatives. Together, we can empower individuals to take small steps that lead to significant change. Join us in creating a greener future for all!
